Adopt a MisFit

Open your heart and home to a cat in need.

How Adoption Works

Our process is designed to find the best match for both you and your new furry family member.

Browse

Check out our available cats and find one (or more!) that catches your eye.

Apply

Fill out our adoption application. We'll review it and reach out to chat about your home and lifestyle.

Meet & Greet

Spend time with your potential new companion to make sure it's a perfect fit.

Welcome Home

Once approved, complete the adoption fee and bring your new MisFit home!

What's Included

  • Spay or neuter surgery
  • Up-to-date vaccinations
  • Microchip and registration
  • FIV/FeLV testing
  • Deworming and flea treatment

Adoption Fees

Our adoption fees help cover the cost of veterinary care and rescue operations.

  • Kittens (under 1 year): $125
  • Adults (1-7 years): $100
  • Seniors (7+ years): $75
  • Bonded pairs: Special pricing

Requirements

  • Must be 21 years or older
  • Landlord approval (if renting)
  • All household members must agree
  • Commitment to indoor-only living
  • Vet reference preferred
