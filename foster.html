✅ Foster Agreement

By submitting this application, I affirm that all information provided is true and accurate to the best of my knowledge. I understand that:

  • Foster cats remain the property of Michelle's MisFits at all times.
  • I will provide a safe, clean, indoor environment for all foster cats.
  • Michelle's MisFits covers all veterinary care — I will not take a foster cat to a vet without prior approval except in a life-threatening emergency.
  • I will administer medications and follow care instructions as directed by the rescue.
  • I will transport foster cats to and from vet appointments and adoption events when possible.
  • I will not rehome, give away, or surrender a foster cat — all cats must be returned to Michelle's MisFits.
  • A home visit or virtual home check may be required.

We'll review your application and reach out within 48 hours. Thank you for opening your heart and home!
