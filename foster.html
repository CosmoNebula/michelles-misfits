Become a Foster

Open your home temporarily and save a life permanently.

Why Foster?

Fostering is the backbone of rescue work. Every foster home means another cat saved from the streets or a shelter.

💖

Save Lives

Every foster home opens a spot for us to rescue another cat. You are directly saving lives.

🏠

Healing Environment

Cats recover and socialize better in a home than in a shelter. Your home is medicine.

😻

No Cost to You

We cover all veterinary care, food, litter, and supplies. You provide the love.

Support & Updates

Frequently Asked Questions

How long will I foster?

Foster periods vary from a few weeks to a few months depending on the cat's needs. We work with your schedule and availability.

What if I already have pets?

Many of our fosters have their own pets! We'll help match you with a cat that's compatible with your household.

Can I adopt my foster cat?

Absolutely! "Foster fails" are some of our favorite outcomes. If you fall in love, we'll work with you on the adoption.

What supplies do I need?

We provide food, litter, and any medical supplies. You just need a safe room or space to keep the cat while they settle in.
