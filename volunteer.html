Volunteer With Us

Your time and skills can change a cat's world.

Ways to Volunteer

No matter your skills or availability, there's a way for you to help our MisFits.

📷

Photography

Help our cats get adopted faster with great photos. A good picture can be the difference between overlooked and adopted.

🚗

Transport

Drive cats to vet appointments, foster homes, or adoption events. Reliable transport volunteers are always needed.

🎉

Events

Help organize and run adoption events, fundraisers, and community outreach. Bring your energy and ideas!

💻

Social Media

Share our cats' stories online, manage posts, and help grow our community presence.

🩹

Supply Runs

Help pick up donated food, litter, and supplies and distribute them to foster homes.

💕

Cat Socialization

Spend time with shy or under-socialized cats to help them trust people and prepare for adoption.

Ready to Join the Team?

Reach out to us and we'll find the perfect volunteer role for you.
