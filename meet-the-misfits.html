Meet the MisFits

Each of these special cats is looking for their forever home.

Our available cats change frequently as new rescues come in and happy adoptions go out. Check back often or contact us to ask about a specific cat!

Whiskers

Male • 3 years • Tabby

A gentle giant who loves lap time and chin scratches. Great with other cats.

Luna

Female • 1 year • Black

Playful and curious, Luna is always up for an adventure. She'd love a home with a window perch.

Mango

Male • 5 years • Orange Tabby

This sweet boy is as warm as his name. Mango is calm, affectionate, and loves a good nap.

Mittens

Female • 2 years • Calico

Mittens is sassy and independent but melts when you find her favorite scratch spot.

Shadow

Male • 7 years • Gray

A senior sweetheart who just wants a quiet home and a warm lap to curl up on.

Biscuit

Female • 4 months • Tuxedo

This tiny tuxedo kitten is full of energy and loves to make biscuits on blankets!
