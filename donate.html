Make a Donation

Every dollar goes directly to veterinary care, food, and supplies for our rescue cats. Michelle's MisFits is a 501(c)(3) — your donation is tax-deductible.

$25

Covers a week of food and litter for one foster cat

$50

Covers vaccinations for one cat

$100

Covers a spay or neuter surgery

$250

Covers emergency veterinary care

To donate, please contact us directly or visit our Facebook page for the latest donation links.
