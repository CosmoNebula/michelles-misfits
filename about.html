🐾 How It All Started

A Rescue Built on Love — Not Logistics

Michelle's MisFits didn't start with a business plan or a board meeting. It started the way most rescues do — with one person who couldn't look away. Michelle had always been the one neighbors called when they found a stray, the one who stayed up through the night with sick kittens, the one who said "yes" when everyone else said "that cat is too far gone."

What began as a one-woman mission quickly grew into something bigger. Fosters stepped up. Donations trickled in. Word spread through Medina County that there was someone who would take the cats nobody else wanted — the bottle babies who needed feeding every two hours, the seniors with medical needs, the ferals who just needed patience and time. Michelle never turned one away.

Today, Michelle's MisFits is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit and a fully foster-based rescue. We don't have a shelter building — we have something better: a network of loving foster homes where every cat gets individualized attention, socialization, and medical care. Every penny donated goes directly to the cats. No overhead. No salaries. Just love, vet bills, and second chances.

— Michelle, Founder 💜

💜 What We Stand For

The Heart Behind the Mission

🍼

Bottle Baby Care

We specialize in the tiniest, most vulnerable kittens — the ones who need round-the-clock feedings, warmth, and a whole lot of love to survive. Our trained fosters give bottle babies the fighting chance they deserve.

🏠

Real Foster Homes

No cages. No kennels. Every cat in our care lives in a real home with a dedicated foster family. They sleep on couches, play with toys, and learn to trust humans — so they're ready for their forever family.

🏥

Full Medical Care

Every MisFit receives complete veterinary care — vaccinations, spay/neuter, microchipping, deworming, flea treatment, and any medical attention they need. No corners cut. No cat left untreated.

💛

No Cat Left Behind

We don't cherry-pick the "easy" cats. We take the ones with medical needs, the shy ones, the seniors, the ones other rescues pass on. Every misfit deserves a chance, and we make sure they get one.

🤝

Community First

We believe rescue is a community effort. From our incredible fosters and volunteers to every single person who shares a post or makes a donation — this rescue runs on the power of people who care.

🔍

Careful Matching

We take the time to match every cat with the right family. It's not about speed — it's about finding a home where both the cat and the adopter will thrive for a lifetime together.

Our Promise to Every Misfit

When a cat comes into our care, we make a promise: you will be safe, you will be loved, and we will not stop until you find your forever home. We don't operate on timelines or deadlines — we operate on love. Whether a cat is with us for two weeks or two years, they will receive the same level of care, attention, and dedication. That's the Michelle's MisFits way.
