About Michelle's MisFits

Our story, our mission, and the people who make it happen.

Our Mission

Michelle's MisFits Cat Rescue is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming cats in Medina County, Ohio and surrounding communities.

We believe every cat — regardless of age, health, or history — deserves a chance at a loving home. From tiny orphaned kittens to senior cats surrendered by their families, we open our hearts and foster homes to them all.

Our Story

What started as one woman's passion for helping stray and abandoned cats has grown into a community-driven rescue. Michelle founded MisFits with a simple belief: no cat should be left behind. Today, our network of dedicated fosters, volunteers, and supporters continues that mission every day.

What We Do

  • Rescue cats from shelters, the streets, and owner surrenders
  • Provide veterinary care including spay/neuter, vaccinations, and medical treatment
  • Place cats in loving foster homes until they find their forever families
  • Facilitate adoptions through careful matching and support
  • Educate the community on responsible pet ownership
